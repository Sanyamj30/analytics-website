How do I add items to my cart?
Click the "Add to Cart" button under any product. It will appear in your cart icon at the top.
Can I save items for later?
Yes, use the wishlist button (heart icon) to save products for later viewing.
Do I need an account to order?
You can browse freely, but ordering requires a registered account.
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days. Express options are available at checkout.
Can I track my order?
Yes, tracking links will be emailed after your order is confirmed and shipped.
Do you offer international shipping?
Currently we only ship within India, but we’re working to expand!
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept Razorpay, UPI, credit/debit cards, and net banking.
How can I request a refund?
Go to your orders page and click "Request Refund" under the product you'd like refunded.
Is it safe to pay online?
Absolutely. All transactions are encrypted and processed via Razorpay's secure gateway.
How do I create an account?
Click “Sign Up” on the top right and fill in your details. It takes under a minute!
I forgot my password. What do I do?
Click “Forgot Password” on the login page and follow the steps to reset.
How do I view my past orders?
After logging in, go to "My Account" > "Order History".
Do you have customer support?
Yes! You can contact us via the contact form or at support@malefashion.com.
Can I cancel an order?
Yes, you can cancel any order before it is shipped from the order summary page.
